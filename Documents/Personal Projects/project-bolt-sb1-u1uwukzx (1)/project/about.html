Our Story

Africa Global Coalition (AGC) emerged from a vision to transform how Africa engages with the world. As a nonprofit organization working at the intersection of foreign policy, trade, and development, we serve as a vital bridge between policymakers, corporate leaders, and communities across Africa.

Our Impact Areas

  • Policy Development: We produce comprehensive policy documents and blueprints that address institutional deficits and promote effective governance across Africa.
  • Youth Empowerment: With 60% of Africa's population under 25 years old, we prioritize engaging young people through universities and educational institutions, nurturing tomorrow's leaders.
  • Economic Development: We focus on the informal employment sector, which represents over 80% of total employment, ensuring inclusive growth and sustainable development.
  • Information Sharing: Breaking down barriers through strategic information sharing across different societal paradigms, ensuring no one is left behind.

Our Values

  • Integrity in all our operations
  • Innovation in problem-solving
  • Collaboration across sectors
  • Teamwork in execution
  • Passion for Africa's development
  • Empathetic interactions
